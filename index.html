The General Data Protection Regulation laws (GDPR) mean that we have to make clear to you how we process and store your data.
The only data we retain from you occurs when you become a member of the Friends, and this data is stored safely and securely and will not be shared with any third parties.
The data in question is given to you by us via the membership form, this may or may not include your full name, your email address and your postal address.
If you require any further information, or would like to review the data we have stored on your behalf, please contact info@friendsofwindsormuseum.org.uk.
